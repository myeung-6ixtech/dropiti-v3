# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last updated: March 30, 2026

---

## About Dropiti

### What is Dropiti?
Dropiti is a Hong Kong-based property rental platform that connects tenants and landlords. We make it easy to search verified listings, submit rental offers, communicate directly with property owners, and leave honest reviews — all in one place.

### Who operates Dropiti?
Dropiti is developed by 6ixTech and is a recipient of the **Cyberport Creative Micro Fund (CCMF)**, a programme by Cyberport Hong Kong supporting innovative technology start-ups.

### Is Dropiti only for Hong Kong properties?
Currently, Dropiti focuses primarily on the Hong Kong rental market, though the platform supports listings from multiple regions.

## Accounts & Registration

### How do I create an account?
Click **Sign Up** in the navigation bar and follow the on-screen steps. You can register with your email address and password, or sign in with a supported identity provider such as Google.

### Is there a fee to join?
No — creating an account on Dropiti is completely free for both tenants and landlords.

### How do I reset my password?
Click **Forgot Password** on the sign-in page and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link shortly.

### Can I change my display name or profile photo?
Yes. Go to **Dashboard → Profile → Edit Profile** to update your display name, photo, about section, and other personal details.

## Listings

### Is there a fee to list a property?
Basic property listings are free. Optional paid features may be introduced in the future. Any fees will be clearly communicated before purchase.

### How do I list a property?
After signing in, go to **Dashboard → Add Property** and fill in the property details, upload photos, and set your asking price. Once published, your listing is visible to all users.

### What are the listing guidelines?
All listings must comply with our [Listing Guidelines](/listing-guidelines). This includes accurate descriptions, genuine photos, lawful rental terms, and respectful communication.

### Can I edit or remove a listing after publishing?
Yes. Go to **Dashboard → My Properties** to edit your listing details, mark it as unavailable, or delete it entirely.

## Offers & Negotiations

### How do offers and negotiations work?
Tenants browse listings and submit a rental offer with a proposed price, lease duration, and move-in date. Landlords can **accept**, **reject**, or **counter-offer** with different terms. Once both parties agree, the offer is accepted and the tenancy can proceed.

### Can I make multiple offers?
Each tenant may have one active offer per property at a time. You can have offers on multiple different properties simultaneously.

### What happens if a landlord does not respond?
Offers expire after the window defined in the offer terms. You can withdraw an active offer at any time from **Dashboard → Applications**.

### Are offers legally binding?
Offers on Dropiti represent intent between parties. A formal tenancy agreement should be signed separately before moving in. Dropiti is not a legal service provider.

## Reviews

### How are reviews handled?
After an accepted offer period, both tenants and landlords are eligible to leave a review for each other. Reviews must be honest and comply with our [Listing Guidelines](/listing-guidelines) and [Terms of Service](/terms).

### Can reviews be removed?
Reviews that violate our guidelines (e.g., contain offensive language or false information) may be removed after investigation. Contact support@dropiti.com if you believe a review is inappropriate.

### Is there a time limit for leaving a review?
Yes — a review window is shown in your dashboard after a completed tenancy. Reviews must be submitted within this window.

## Privacy & Advertising

### Does Dropiti show advertisements?
Dropiti may display advertisements served by **Google AdSense**. Ads are only personalised if you have consented to advertising cookies via our cookie consent banner.

### How can I opt out of personalised ads?
You can opt out at any time by visiting [Google's Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) or by clearing site data for dropiti.com in your browser, which will reset your cookie consent choice.

### How does Dropiti handle my personal data?
Please read our full [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for details on what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Support

### Who can I contact for support?
Email our support team at **support@dropiti.com**. Please include your user ID (found in **Dashboard → Settings**) and a brief description of your issue.

### How do I report a suspicious listing or user?
If you believe a listing or user is fraudulent or in violation of our guidelines, please email support@dropiti.com with the relevant listing or user link and details of your concern.
