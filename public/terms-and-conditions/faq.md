# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last updated: 2025-01-01

## What is Dropiti?
Dropiti is a platform that connects tenants and landlords to list, discover, and manage property rentals.

## How do I create an account?
Click Sign Up in the header and follow the on-screen steps. You can register with email/password or a supported identity provider.

## Is there a fee to list a property?
Basic listing is free. Optional paid features may be available in the future. Any fees will be clearly shown before purchase.

## How do offers and negotiations work?
Tenants submit offers to landlords. Landlords can accept, reject, or counter. Once accepted, both parties can leave reviews within the review window.

## How are reviews handled?
Reviews can be written within the review window shown in your dashboard. Reviews must comply with our Listing Guidelines and Terms of Service.

## Who can I contact for support?
Email support at support@dropiti.com. Please include your user ID and a short description of your issue.
