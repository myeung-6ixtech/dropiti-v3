# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: January 1, 2025*

## Overview

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use Dropiti.

## Information We Collect

### Account Data
- **Name and contact information** (email, phone number)
- **Profile photo** and display name
- **Account preferences** and settings

### Usage Data
- **Pages visited** and features used
- **Actions taken** on the platform
- **Search queries** and filters applied

### Device Data
- **Browser type** and version
- **Operating system** and device type
- **IP address** and location data

## How We Use Information

### Service Operation
- **Operate and improve** the Dropiti platform
- **Provide customer support** and communications
- **Prevent fraud and abuse** on the platform

### Personalization
- **Customize content** and recommendations
- **Improve user experience** based on usage patterns
- **Send relevant notifications** and updates

## Information Sharing

### We Do Not Sell Personal Data
Your personal information is never sold to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Service Providers
We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the platform:
- **Payment processors** for secure transactions
- **Cloud hosting** for data storage
- **Analytics services** for platform improvement

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption** of sensitive data
- **Regular security audits** and updates
- **Access controls** and authentication

### No Guarantee
While we implement reasonable safeguards, no method of data transmission is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your account and data
- **Export** your data in a portable format

### Contact Information
For privacy-related questions: **privacy@dropiti.com**

## Policy Updates

### Notification Process
- **Material changes** will be notified via the app
- **Regular updates** posted on this page
- **Email notifications** for significant changes

### Continued Use
Using Dropiti after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
