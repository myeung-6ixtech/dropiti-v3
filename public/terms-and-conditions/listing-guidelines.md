# Listing Guidelines

*Last updated: January 1, 2025*

## Overview

These guidelines ensure all property listings on Dropiti maintain high quality standards and provide accurate, helpful information to potential tenants.

## Core Requirements

### 1. Accuracy
- Provide **accurate, up-to-date details** for your property
- Misleading or false information is strictly prohibited
- Update information promptly when changes occur

### 2. Photos
- Use **clear, recent photos** you own the rights to
- No watermarks, logos, or offensive content
- Show all major areas: living spaces, bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen
- Ensure good lighting and proper angles

### 3. Prohibited Content
- **No discriminatory, hateful, or illegal content**
- No infringing material or copyright violations
- No spam, misleading claims, or inappropriate language

### 4. Pricing
- Set **transparent pricing** with no hidden fees
- Include applicable fees or clearly disclose them in the description
- Specify what's included in the rent (utilities, parking, etc.)

### 5. Communication
- Be **responsive and respectful** to all inquiries
- Provide clear, helpful responses within 24 hours
- Off-platform transactions are discouraged for safety

### 6. Compliance
- Ensure your listing complies with **local laws and regulations**
- Follow fair housing laws and anti-discrimination policies
- Respect zoning and rental regulations

### 7. Moderation
We reserve the right to:
- Edit listings for clarity and accuracy
- Hide listings that violate guidelines
- Remove listings that repeatedly violate policies
- Suspend accounts for serious violations

## Best Practices

### Property Descriptions
- Be detailed but concise
- Highlight unique features and amenities
- Use clear, professional language
- Include relevant property history

### Photo Quality
- Take photos during daylight hours
- Clean and declutter before photographing
- Show both close-up and wide-angle views
- Include exterior shots when possible

### Response Time
- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
- Be available for property viewings
- Provide clear next steps for interested tenants

## Need Help?

If you have questions about these guidelines or need assistance with your listing, please contact our support team. We're here to help you create successful, compliant property listings.
