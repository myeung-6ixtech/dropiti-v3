## Listing Guidelines

Last updated: 2025-01-01

1. Accuracy
Provide accurate, up-to-date details for your property. Misleading or false information is prohibited.

2. Photos
Use clear, recent photos you own the rights to. No watermarks or offensive content.

3. Prohibited Content
No discriminatory, hateful, illegal, or infringing content.

4. Pricing
Set transparent pricing. Include applicable fees or clearly disclose them in the description.

5. Communication
Be responsive and respectful to inquiries. Off-platform transactions are discouraged.

6. Compliance
Ensure your listing complies with local laws and regulations.

7. Moderation
We may edit, hide, or remove listings that violate these guidelines.
