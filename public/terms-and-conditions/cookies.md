## Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve Dropiti.

1. What Are Cookies?
Small text files stored on your device to remember settings and preferences.

2. Types of Cookies
- Essential: required for core functionality
- Analytics: help us understand usage
- Preferences: remember choices

3. Managing Cookies
You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.
